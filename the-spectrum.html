The Enfys Spectrum

Enfys means rainbow, and it is also the methodology spine, the thing that makes PowerVox credible, repeatable and genuinely transformational, not just beautiful. Seven colours, seven stages. Each one is a psychological shift, a leadership capability and a practice, and each is anchored to a place in the village.

1

Curiosity

The Crossing
From closed, certain, guarded  →  to open, questioning, coachable
"What if there's more available to you than this?"
Core skill

Expanding perspective; suspending judgement. Without curiosity, nothing moves.

Practice

The 10 Better Questions drill; the Future Self interview; a curiosity audit, where am I defaulting to certainty?

2

Welcome

The Threshold · Aur
From guarded, unsafe, performative  →  to safe, seen, able to soften
"You don't find your voice in a place that feels unsafe."
Core skill

Creating safety, for self and others; regulating the nervous system. Psychological safety, done beautifully and on purpose.

Practice

The state check-in (head, heart, body); safety mapping, where do I shrink versus expand; circle agreements for groups.

The welcome book. At the threshold sits a book. You write one true thing, where you have come from, a truth you are ready to acknowledge, and you are handed a pen, not as a tool but as a symbol. Aur, soft gold, is the word you are greeted with once you have crossed. ⚑ Canon, from Rachel's World of Enfys.

3

Growth

The Green
From a fixed identity  →  to evolving, self-led growth
"You are allowed to become."
Core skill

Self-awareness; identity expansion. Where people start to re-author themselves, the Book of Future You.

Practice

Identity rewrite ("I am someone who…"); limiting-belief excavation; growth-edge mapping, where am I ready but resisting?

4

Truth

The River
From filtered, diluted, people-pleasing  →  to clear, honest, aligned
"Say what is true, not what is safe."
Core skill

Clarity of thought; clean communication. Where voice starts to sharpen.

Practice

The Say It Cleanly drill, truth in one sentence; the Unsaid List; truth versus story, separating fact from interpretation.

5

Courage

The Town Hall
From silence, hesitation  →  to expression, leadership, visibility
"Your voice matters. Use it."  A PowerVox core moment.
Core skill

Speaking under pressure; visible leadership.

Practice

Power Room simulations; the difficult-conversation framework; 3-minute stand and speak, no prep; the Podium, where members book in to practise and get feedback.

6

Insight

The Burrows
From surface-level action  →  to deep integration and wisdom
"Pause. Listen. There is more underneath."
Core skill

Reflection; pattern recognition; meditation for mindfulness and intuition. The stage most programmes skip; here it is made foundational.

Practice

Reflective journaling prompts; pattern mapping, where does this show up across my life; intuitive-decision practice, head versus instinct.

7

Community

The Gather
From isolated leadership  →  to connected, collective power
"Together we rise."  Where transformation becomes sustainable.
Core skill

Relational leadership; contribution; peer support and inspiration.

Practice

Witnessing circles, listening without fixing; hot-seat coaching; the contribution challenge, how do I lift others?

Why it's a methodology, not just a model

Most people stop at a nice model. The Spectrum keeps people moving between the stages: it is not a ladder you climb once and leave. A leader will speak with courage and then need to return to safety; find truth and then need fresh curiosity. That ongoing movement, the transformation cycle practised through Enfys play, is what turns a framework into a method. It is the same non-linearity the six dimensions hold: you do not finish the rainbow, you live inside it. ⚑ Current articulation of Rachel's transformation model; the colour-to-stage mapping is hers to confirm.
