The Enterprise Engagement

How PowerVox sells into an organisation. The other road in, beside the self-serve funnel: a premium, outcome-led journey bought by a CPO or a board, that takes a leadership team from a baseline to visible, observable change in the rooms that matter.

There are two roads into PowerVox. One is the self-serve funnel, the Black Egg drawing individuals up the value ladder. This is the other: an organisational engagement, sold to a buyer with a budget and a team, structured so it feels tight, premium and outcome-led. Most of the time it is a three-to-four phase journey.
The journey
Phase 1Diagnosis & Discovery4–6 weeks
"Where are your leaders actually on the Voice Ladder?"
What we do

A Voice Quotient diagnostic for each leader, assessed across Control → Clarity → Influence, message landing and vocal patterns, through surveys, short recorded speaking samples and optional observation of real meetings. The first four training modules are delivered live and online.

What the client gets

A clear baseline of communication capability, and the patterns named: the invisible leaders (high capability, low voice), the dominant voices (high airtime, low impact), and systemic risks like a silence culture.

Deliverable · the Executive Insight Report. A board-level narrative: here is what your leaders are saying, here is what is not landing, here is the cost. This phase alone is a valuable insight, and it is where PowerVox differentiates from almost the entire market.
Phase 2Design2–3 weeks
"What specifically needs to shift, and how?"
What we do

Translate the diagnostic into a bespoke Voice Ladder pathway: target behaviours at each level, priority groups (executive, senior, emerging leaders), and the high-stakes scenarios that matter (boardrooms, change comms).

What the client gets

A tailored intervention plan, not generic training, with clear linkage to business outcomes: retention, decision quality, change execution.

Deliverable · the PowerVox Programme Blueprint, with the quote for the phase ahead: the learning architecture, the measurement framework, the behaviour-tracking approach. This is where the buyer's fear is de-risked, by following a methodology.
Phase 3Activation & Embed8–16 weeks
"Move leaders up the Voice Ladder, and make it stick."
What we do

A core programme in small cohorts: saying the thing (Control → Clarity), landing the message (Clarity → Influence), handling challenge and tension, in real scenarios. Live application in real meetings and town halls. Power Room simulations, optional one-to-one coaching, and the Greenhouse for senior women where it fits.

What the client gets

Visible, observable behaviour change. Not "they felt more confident," but "they ran the meeting differently, the message landed, the team responded." Tracked weekly: what did you say differently, and what happened as a result?

Deliverable · the Behaviour Evidence Portfolio. Before-and-after examples, observed shifts, and optional short video comparisons, which are very powerful for boards. "You can't put communication change on a spreadsheet, but you can show it in a room."
Phase 4 · optionalSustain & Scaleongoing
"Make this part of how leadership works here."
What we do

The longer-term revenue and real-impact layer: keep the capability alive across the organisation through a retainer, refreshers, new cohorts and ongoing measurement, so the change holds rather than fading after the programme.

What the client gets

Communication treated as standing leadership infrastructure, not a one-off intervention, with the evidence base compounding over time.
