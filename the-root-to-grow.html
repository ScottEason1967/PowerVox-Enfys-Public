What it is

The engine of the year. Root to Grow is where a leader begins the work: initiation, awareness, the shared language of Voice Intelligence, and a baseline they can measure everything else against. It is deliberately the opening stretch of the Greenhouse rather than a standalone course, so the people who finish it are already standing at the door of the recurring programme. Cohorts are small, ten to twelve, and delivered live by Rachel and Hannah together. That is a choice, not a constraint dressed up as one: the count is set by what two people can deliver well, because the early cohorts are built for quality over volume. The aim is raving fans who carry on, not a big first number.

It is also how PowerVox earns the right to its category claim without spending founder time it does not have. Every cohort runs the full VQ Assessment and the Black Egg as part of the method, so the participants are paying clients and the highest-quality evidence source at the same time. We know the leader and we know their context. The research is generated as a by-product of normal trading, not in competition with it.
