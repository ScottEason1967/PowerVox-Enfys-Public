The Construct

PowerVox is building a category, not competing in one. Category creators win partly by owning specific words and using them with absolute consistency. This is the canonical home of the estate's language — when any document drifts, this is what it is drifting from.

PowerVox develops Voice Intelligence — measured by Voice Quotient™ — which produces clearer, better-landed communication, whose hypothesised outcomes are lower compensation, reduced burnout risk and stronger psychological safety.
Capability → communication → outcomes. We never drop the middle term.

The four layers · where a voice breaks down

The six dimensions are what the instrument scores. Beneath them runs a second lens: the four sequential, interdependent points where a voice succeeds or breaks down. A voice that does not land is rarely failing at expression alone; most often the breakdown is further back, at perception or integration, and the silence at the surface is only the symptom. Two lenses on one capability, the layers explain where it breaks, the dimensions are what is measured.

1 · PERCEPTION

Where it begins. Voice Intelligence starts earlier than most frameworks, at the moment something is noticed. What you fail to perceive, you cannot integrate. This receptive capacity is named Signal.

2 · INTEGRATION

Holding thinking and feeling at once. Registering both the room and the report, the data and the discomfort, without flattening one to manage the other.

3 · EXPRESSION

Articulation under pressure. Clarity not as polish, but as faithfulness to what was perceived and integrated.

4 · LANDING

Whether what you mean survives the journey into someone else. Coherence, the match between what is known inside and what is said outside, is what makes a voice land, and what makes its absence felt before it is named.

The triadic structure · what fails, what it costs, what restores

The four compensations · outward

What the leader does in the room — visible to others. The compensations cost the room.

  • Over-control
  • Masking
  • Cognitive overload
  • Relational disconnection

The seven leaks · inward

What happens in the leader — often invisible even to themselves. The leaks cost the leader. (A sub-term under Compensation, held for elevation once the seven have stabilised.)

  • Self-doubt
  • Over-responsibility
  • Shame
  • Hyper-reflection
  • Perfectionism
  • Nervous-system overload
  • Disconnected signal
Anchors — the way back.

Small, deliberate, portable practices by which leaders return to VQ between sessions: a phrase, gesture, image or ritual carried out of the room. "These are the great days" carved at the Enfys threshold is a gratitude-reframe; Aur — thumb and ring finger pressed together — is a somatic anchor; the garlands chosen at the close of each Gather are anchors of intention. Not affirmations: interventions with measurable mechanisms, and the part of the methodology with the strongest existing evidence base.

The second variable · the room

Everything above measures the voice. But a voice that lands is the voice meeting the room, and the room is the second variable the method works on. Same person, same voice, same hour: supplicant in one room, authority in the next. The only thing that changed was who held the power, and what that power was built to receive. So when a capable voice does not land, locating the whole problem in the speaker is incomplete by our own analysis. The conditions are the variable, not the person.

VQ scores the speaker. Its complement, the Conditions Taxonomy, maps the room across twelve dimensions: where power sits, what the room is built to receive, who is capable and unheard inside it, and whether the room is willing to change. The two instruments together are the diagnosis, and the diagnosis is the gap between a high-VQ voice and a low-reception room. The full schema and the canon that unites both sit on the Instrument.

The vocabulary of the room.

Three terms are defined here as canon and explained in full where they live, so this page stays the dictionary and not a second strategy. Re-water the voice (the Greenhouse) is the lever that works on the person. Change the room (the allyship pathway) is the lever that works on the conditions, the half that makes the offer true rather than complicit. The readiness gate is the honest fork that asks whether the people who hold the power will learn to receive differently, and so decides which engagement is real. The strategy that sequences them, women first toward a gender-neutral destination, lives on the Greenhouse and, for a prospect, on the Front Door; the schema and the canon sit on the Instrument. PowerVox maps conditions; it is not a women's-empowerment company.

The staged hypothesis · the claims discipline

Test short chains before elegant theories. State only the link currently being tested. The word 'mediate' is reserved for the causal stage — using it early is the fastest way to lose an academic's trust.

Phase Zero (2026 beta): lower receptive VQ (Signal) is associated with elevated compensation behaviours — correlational, one link. Phase One (2027–28): compensation behaviours associated with increased burnout risk and reduced safety indicators — correlational, second link. Mechanism (2028 pilot+): compensation mediates the relationship between receptive VQ and organisational outcomes — causal. Outcome domains: burnout risk and leader-perceived psychological safety are measured now; leadership sustainability proxy-only; organisational trust reserved for multi-source work. The diagnostic measures the capability and its measure — never the outcomes. That line is what stops the category over-claiming before it has earned its evidence.
