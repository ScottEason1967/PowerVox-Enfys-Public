The settlement · one estate, two operating models, one method
The Loveheart TOM · the ecology

How the whole ecology operates as a living system in service of the destination. Its why is the destination statement; its actors are the elements of the ecology, not roles in a company. It holds what the chassis would otherwise drop: the three books, the foundations, Arte Club (held in paused intention), the research integrity standard — and Enfys itself, the architecture lived as story.

The PowerVox TOM · the chassis — nested, never parallel

How a three-person team runs a multi-stream commercial business predictably, without living in Rachel's head. Its why is commercial: revenue, evidence, category on one path. Its actors are the four seats; its processes are the engagement lifecycle, the consent–IP–data subsystem, qualification and cadence protection. Clean, conventional shape — the Cartographer's home ground.

the Loop · the instrument

Separate from both operating models: the generic, client-agnostic elicitation engine that can produce any operating model — including these two. The models are content; the method is the instrument that draws content out of a subject.
